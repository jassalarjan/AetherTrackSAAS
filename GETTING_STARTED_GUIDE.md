# Getting Started Guide

Welcome to AetherTrack. This guide walks you through the initial setup process so you can start using the platform effectively from day one.

---

## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document provides step-by-step instructions for new users setting up their AetherTrack account for the first time. It covers account creation, workspace setup, and the essential actions you need to take to begin using the platform. Use this guide as a reference during your onboarding process.

---

## 2. Who Should Read This Document

This guide is intended for:

- New employees joining an organization that uses AetherTrack
- Team members invited to join an existing workspace
- Administrators setting up their first AetherTrack account
- Anyone new to the platform who needs to complete initial setup

---

## 3. Before You Begin

Before starting the setup process, ensure you have the following:

- **A valid email address** — You will use this to create your account and receive verification emails.
- **Access credentials** — If you were invited by an administrator, you should have received an invitation email with a link or your team may have pre-created your account.
- **A supported web browser** — AetherTrack works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.
- **Approximate time** — Completing all setup steps takes approximately 10-15 minutes.

---

## 4. Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Access the Platform

Open your web browser and navigate to the AetherTrack platform URL provided by your administrator. The login page will display options to sign in or create a new account.

**What to expect:** The login page shows fields for email and password, along with options to create an account or reset a password.

### Step 2: Create Your Account or Accept an Invitation

If you received an invitation from your workspace administrator, click the invitation link in your email. This link may direct you to create an account or prompt you to set your password if an account was pre-created for you.

If you are setting up an account without an invitation, look for a "Create Account" or "Sign Up" option on the login page. Enter your email address and any other required information provided by your organization.

**What to expect:** After providing your email, you will either receive a verification email or be prompted to proceed to the next step depending on your organization's settings.

### Step 3: Verify Your Email

Check your email inbox for a verification message from AetherTrack. Click the verification link included in the email. If you do not see the email, check your spam or junk folder.

**What to expect:** A confirmation message indicating your email has been verified. The link typically expires after 24 hours. If it has expired, you may need to request a new verification email.

### Step 4: Set Your Password

Create a secure password for your account. Your organization may have specific password requirements such as minimum length, special characters, or numbers. Follow the guidelines displayed on the password setup screen.

**What to expect:** A password field and confirmation field. Ensure both entries match. You will see a strength indicator as you type.

### Step 5: Complete Your Profile

After setting your password, you may be prompted to complete your user profile. This typically includes:

- Your full name
- Job title or role
- Department or team
- Profile photo (optional)

Fill in the requested information. Some fields may be required while others are optional.

**What to expect:** A form with several fields. Your profile helps your teammates identify you and enables features like attendance tracking and task assignments.

### Step 6: Join or Create Your First Workspace

AetherTrack organizes work into workspaces. Depending on your situation, you will either join an existing workspace or create a new one.

**If you were invited:** You will see a prompt to join a specific workspace. Accept the invitation to gain access.

**If you are an administrator creating a workspace:** You will be asked to choose a workspace type:

- **CORE workspace** — Unlimited users, tasks, and teams. Best for organizations that need full functionality.
- **COMMUNITY workspace** — Limited to 10 users, 100 tasks, and 3 teams. Suitable for small teams or pilot projects.

Enter a name for your workspace and complete any additional configuration steps required by your organization.

**What to expect:** A screen where you either accept an invitation or create a new workspace. The workspace name you choose will be visible to all members.

### Step 7: Explore the Dashboard

Once your account and workspace are set up, you will be directed to the main dashboard. Take a moment to familiarize yourself with the layout.

The dashboard typically includes:

- **Navigation menu** — Access different sections like Projects, Tasks, HR, Teams, and Analytics.
- **Quick actions** — Buttons for common tasks like creating a new task or checking in.
- **Recent activity** — A feed of recent updates relevant to you.
- **Notifications bell** — Alerts for items requiring your attention.

**What to expect:** Your main hub for the platform. Spend a few minutes clicking through the navigation menu to see what is available.

## 5. Key First Actions

After completing setup, complete these essential actions to begin using the platform effectively.

### Checking In to Attendance

If your organization uses attendance tracking:

1. Look for a "Check In" button on your dashboard or navigate to the HR section.
2. Click the button to record your arrival. Some organizations may require check-in at specific times.
3. You can also check your attendance history from the HR or Attendance section.

**What to expect:** A confirmation that your check-in was recorded. You may see your current status as "Checked In" or similar.

### Creating Your First Task

1. Navigate to the Tasks section from the main menu.
2. Click "New Task" or "Create Task."
3. Enter a title for the task.
4. Assign a status (To Do, In Progress, Review, Done) and set a priority if applicable.
5. Assign the task to yourself or a team member.
6. Add a due date if the task has a deadline.
7. Click "Save" to create the task.

**What to expect:** The task appears in your task list or board view. You can update it at any time.

### Viewing Your Team

1. Go to the Teams section in the navigation menu.
2. Select your team from the list.
3. View team members, their roles, and current task assignments.

**What to expect:** A list of team members with their names, roles, and current status. This helps you understand who is working on what.

### Checking Your Leave Balance

1. Navigate to the HR section, then to Leave or Time Off.
2. Look for a summary of your leave balances, which typically shows available days for each leave type (annual leave, sick leave, personal days).
3. You can also see a history of leave requests and their status.

**What to expect:** A breakdown of your leave entitlements, used days, and remaining balance.

## 6. Understanding Notifications

AetherTrack keeps you informed through a notification system. Notifications appear in the bell icon in the top navigation bar and may include:

- **Task updates** — When a task is assigned to you, updated, or completed.
- **Leave requests** — Status updates on leave requests you submitted or need to approve.
- **Attendance alerts** — Reminders to check in or notifications about attendance issues.
- **Team activity** — Updates about team members joining, leaving, or changing roles.
- **Mentions** — When someone mentions you in a comment or task.

Click the notification bell to see all your notifications. Click on any notification to navigate to the relevant item. You can mark notifications as read or archive them to keep your list organized.

## 8. Common First-Time Questions

**What if I forget my password?**
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it. You will receive an email with a reset link.

**What if I do not receive the verification email?**
Check your spam or junk folder. If you still do not see it, contact your administrator to request a new verification email.

**Can I change my workspace name after creation?**
Workspace names can typically be changed by workspace administrators through the workspace settings.

**How do I switch between workspaces?**
If you have access to multiple workspaces, use the workspace switcher in the top navigation bar to change between them.

**What browser should I use?**
AetherTrack supports the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Using an outdated browser may limit functionality.

**Who do I contact for help?**
Contact your workspace administrator or IT support team for assistance. They can help with account issues, permissions, and platform configuration.

**Can I use AetherTrack on mobile?**
AetherTrack is optimized for desktop browsers. Mobile access may be available depending on your organization's configuration.
